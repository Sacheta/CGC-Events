👨‍🏫 Interactive Mentoring Session on "SUCCESSFUL START-UP FOUNDERS"
🗓️ Date:- 13th February, 2021
⏰ Time:- 11:00 AM - 12:00 NOON
👩‍💻 Venue:- On Google meet

Outcomes:

• The expert gave a lot of information about certain firms which failed pertaining to certain aspects and whose innovative ideas were taken away by the other firms and turned into a huge success just by analyzing the market conditions and demands rightly.

• The expert also talked about the ideas available on social media and how to turn them up to successful start – ups by just giving them a right direction and a thoughtful process.

• He also talked about the resources present on sites like you tube and search engines like google which might help the students to get a clear idea of what entrepreneurship is all about and to think like the successful entrepreneurs think.

• Students got a clarity about establishing a successful startup by conducting rigorous market analysis, finding problems that are worth solving, identifying financing options, brand placement and generating revenue through their business ideas.
